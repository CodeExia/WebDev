Improvised Shawarma Recipe

This is my girlfriend's recipe for an Improvised Shawarma! Ever feel like making your own shawarma but lack some crucical and not easily accessible (for Filipinos) ingredients? Well, you're in luck! Read more to know more.

Ingredients:

  • Pita bread (homemade or storebought)
  • White onions
  • Cucumber
  • Tomato
  • Shawarma Sauce (Tutorial below)

How to make Shawarma Sauce:

  • 1 piece of garlic
  • Pinch of pepper
  • 1 cup of mayonaise
  • 1 tablespoon of gochujang
  • 1/2 cup of yogurt
  • 1/4 cup of water
